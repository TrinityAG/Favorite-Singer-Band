Kurt Cobain

About Kurt

Born in Aberdeen, Washington, kurt Donald cobain formed the band Nirvana With Krist Novoselic and Aaron Burckhard in 1987 and basically created grunge music. He would later go on to marry Courtney Love, then succumb to his battle with depression and drug use and commit suicide in 1994.

**All information is from Wikiapedia**

Fun Facts

  • The band was meant as a joke, til someone asked them if they'd record their songs (or something like that, haha).
  • The name Nirvana comes from the Buddhist concept, and means "freedom from pain, suffering, and the external world".
  • The genre of the band is punk rock.
  • When he was younger, Kurt was inspired by bands such as The Beatles (especially John Lennon), and 7o's hard rock and heavy metal bands such as Led Zepplin, AC/DC, Black Sabbath, Aerosmith, Queen, and Kiss.
  • Kurt died in 1994.
